# Small Farm Planner Social Copy

## Positioning

Small Farm Planner is a game-like garden planner for backyard growers. It lets people plan a real vegetable garden with the clarity of a cozy farm game and the logic of companion planting, crop rotation, weather tasks, harvest tracking, and next-season planning.

## Short Launch Post

I am building Small Farm Planner, a game-like planning tool for backyard gardens.

Instead of a spreadsheet or static diagram, you can lay out crops on a grid, see companion planting conflicts, handle garden tasks, record harvests, clean up beds, and plan the next season.

It is still an early alpha, but the first 3-minute guided experience is ready.

If you grow vegetables, herbs, flowers, or just like garden planning tools, I would love feedback.

## LinkedIn Version

I have been prototyping a product at the intersection of game-like interfaces and practical home gardening: Small Farm Planner.

The idea is simple: backyard growers should be able to plan a real garden with the clarity and delight of a farm game, while still getting useful agronomic guidance.

The current alpha includes:
- A grid-based garden planner
- Companion planting and conflict feedback
- Mock weather-driven tasks
- Harvest logging
- Bed cleanup and next-season crop suggestions
- A 3-minute guided tester flow

I am now looking for a small first round of testers, especially people who grow vegetables, herbs, or flowers at home.
